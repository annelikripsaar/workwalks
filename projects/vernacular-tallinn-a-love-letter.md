  Text: Vernacular design is: amateur, local, everyday design,. It's something that you can find everywhere you look in the city like in: signs of beauty salons signs, shop opening hours on the door, note of changed opening times on the same door, price tags for market strawberries. However, that all is usually excluded from professional discourse and treated . Llike a slang of visual culture. Mainstream ideals such as beauty, luxury,diligencealso diligenceand professionalism on the signage of small businesses seem distorted since, sincere ambitions often end up being a bit awkward according to the rules of conventionally understood good design. However, the multiplicity of voices and the diversity of visions are also a sign of good health of the system. It's time to bring hairdresser and pawnshop signs to the (modest) spotlight!




  In my graduation project I try to approach the array of vernacular design in different ways by creating , doing so in several different categories. The ten zines I have designed show in both playful and more serious ways on how to ofexperience an urbanexperience ofing the urbanenvironment, how to value and find in an environment that is increasingly distant something that is sinceritye, humanity, immediacyte, warmth inthese increasingly distant surroundings.

Tekst: Vernakulaardisain ehk amatöör-, igapäeva-, argidisain. See on miski, mida leidub linnaruumis, nagu näiteks igal pool kuhu vaatad: ilusalongide sildid, keldriäri avamisajad uksel, muutunud avamisaegade teavitus samal uksel, turumaasikate hinnasildid. Need näitedKuid siiski jäävadb see reeglina erialasest diskursusest välja olles . Jjustkui visuaalkultuuri släng. Peavoolu ideaalid nagu ilu, luksuslikkus, samuti töökus ja professionaalsus on väikeäride siltidel justkui kõverpeeglis, kuna need siirad taotlused on kukkunud tihti konventsionaalselt hea disaini kontekstiskohaselt kohmakalt välja kukkunud. Kuid häälte paljusus ning nägemuste mitmekesisus on ka märk süsteemi heast tervisest. On aeg juuksuri- ja pandimajasildid (tagasihoidlikku) rambivalgusesse tuua!



Oma lõputöös üritangi vernakulaardisainimassiivi eri külgedelt järada, tehes seda läbi mitme erineva kategooria abil. Kümnes lühitrükises on toodud välja nii mängulisemad kui tõsisemad viisid, kuidas linnaümbrust kogeda, kuidas väärtustada ja leida aina kaugemaks muutuvast ümbrusest seda, mis on siiras, inimlik, vahetu, soe.
